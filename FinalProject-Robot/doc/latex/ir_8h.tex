Get the most recent I\+R measurement

The I\+R A\+P\+I is completely asynchronous. The A\+D\+C is continuously sampled. \hyperlink{ir_8h_aeb4a7b0af8a48c45806629488f6e05e1}{I\+R\+\_\+\+Get\+Data()} simply returns the most recent sample. \hyperlink{ir_8h_a47f3603c429f96a48f77b05fed02f18b}{I\+R\+\_\+\+Get\+Data\+Blocking()} blocks until new data is available before returning.
